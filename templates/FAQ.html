FAQs

What is the purpose of this system?

This system allows users to manage pets, adopters, and adoption records efficiently.

How can I add a new adopter?

Navigate to the "Manage Adopters" page, fill in the required details in the form, and click "Add Adopter."

Can I delete an adopter?

Yes! Use the "Delete" button next to the adopter's details on the "Adopters List" section.
